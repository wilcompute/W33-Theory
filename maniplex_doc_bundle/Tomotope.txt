The Tomotope is an isogonal abstract polychoron.

Rank: 4
Type: Isogonal
Cells: 4 tetrahedra, 4 hemioctahedra
Faces: 16 triangles
Edges: 12
Vertices: 4
Vertex figure: Hemicuboctahedron
Flag count: 192
Orientable: No
Symmetry: M_2Â , order 96
Flag orbits: 2
The tomotope is notable in that its monodromy group does not satisfy the intersection condition and thus is not an abstract polytope. As a result the tomotope has infinitely many minimal regular covers, all of type {3,12,4}. It does not satisfy the intersection condition and thus is not a C-group. As a result the minimal regular cover of the tomotope is not an abstract regular polytope (but rather a maniplex).
